About

Communicating a mindset here! Ynat started in 2020 with Melanie's passion for upcycled accessories and unique thrift pieces.

After three years of wild experimenting, she came full circle, focusing on what screams "Melanie would wear that." Exclusive, upcycled eyewear was the next project because who doesn’t want to rock a pair of shades that scream individuality?

What if you could see the world through nature’s lens? Would it feel as peaceful as it looks? Our collections draw from nature’s gentle hues, offering bold, vibrant shades—one unique piece per collection. Why just one? Because aren’t we all tired of the copy-paste culture?

As a startup with a point to prove, we aim to be the go-to store for your standout moments and experiencing life through your own lens.

Why sunglasses?

Sunglasses are the ultimate expression of character —whether you're sunbathing, hiking, hitting the slopes, sipping coffee, walking around, eating by the sidewalk, stuck in traffic, hitting the dance floor, or just hiding those cranky, tired eyes after a wild night out.
