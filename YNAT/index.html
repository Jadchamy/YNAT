FAQ

Are the sunglasses brand new?

While the materials and frames are vintage, each pair is redesigned and repurposed, making them unique but not brand new.


Do you offer UV protection?

Yes, all our sunglasses are optometrist-approved and come with UV protection, including 400 UV protection. This means they block 100% of UVA and UVB rays, offering superior protection from the sun's harmful rays.


Are these sunglasses sustainable?

Absolutely! By upcycling vintage frames, we reduce waste and promote environmental consciousness.


How do I care for my upcycled sunglasses?

Handle with care, clean with a soft cloth, and avoid harsh chemicals to maintain their unique look and longevity.


Can I customize my sunglasses?

Each pair is already uniquely designed, but you can reach out for specific requests or adjustments.


Refund Policy

We do not offer refunds unless the sunglasses have arrived broken. Please email us at oynat.store@gmail.com to report broken or damaged items.
